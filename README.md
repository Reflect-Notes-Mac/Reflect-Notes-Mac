## Overview

Reflect Notes Professional Suite is a complete knowledge and writing platform that serves users who want their note-taking to be both deeply connected and AI-assisted, with the privacy guarantee of end-to-end encryption protecting everything they write.

Calendar integration pulls meeting notes and daily entries into the note timeline, connecting what happens in your calendar with the notes you take about it and building a chronological record of your working life alongside the topical knowledge graph.

Kindle and Readwise integration imports highlights and annotations from books and articles directly into your note library, making your reading highlights part of the connected knowledge graph rather than isolated in a separate reading app.

Collaboration tools allow notes to be shared with specific people for review or co-editing, enabling the same networked note approach to be applied to collaborative documents and team knowledge bases.

Reflect Notes Professional Suite is the note-taking platform for users who want their thinking to be both well-connected and genuinely private, with AI assistance that accelerates writing without compromising the security of their ideas.

## Performance & Integration

Reflect Notes is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows

## System Requirements

- macOS 12 Monterey or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel — Universal Binary
- Works with macOS Ventura, Sonoma and Sequoia
